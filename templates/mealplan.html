Meal Plan Levels Explanation
  • Gold: Indicates a meal plan that exists in the NHANES dataset and is consumed by users who are within their personal nutritional target range.
  • Silver: Indicates a meal plan that exists in the NHANES dataset and meets personal nutritional target range of a different user.
  • Bronze: Indicates a meal plan that does not exist in the NHANES dataset and is generated using a genetic algorithm from the NHANES food library based on the user's nutritional target range.
